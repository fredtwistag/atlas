Sara 9:22 AM

Sure. So when a request comes in via Salesforce, the SDR triages it and assigns it to one of the AEs. The AE pulls together a draft using our SKU pricing sheet, but for enterprise deals there's almost always custom pricing involved — that goes to João for approval.

Then it bounces between AE, João, and sometimes finance for 2-4 days before we send anything to the customer.

A
Atlas 9:23 AM

Got it. You mentioned João for pricing approval — is that always the same person, and how long does it usually take him to turn it around?

S
Sara 9:24 AM

Always João. He's the VP Sales. Turnaround depends — if he's in the office, same day. If he's traveling, can be 2 days. And honestly when he's slammed, sometimes AEs just send the quote at list price and tell the customer "we'll come back with a discount" — which is messy because then the customer anchors on list and we lose negotiating room.
