Questions.

Why is there no monthly subscription?
Because "persistent" requires Slack/Teams integration to be honest. Until that ships (Q3 2026 target), we sell the Sprint — a focused 3-4 week discovery engagement with a defined output. You'll always know what you're getting.
What if my team won't engage with the bot?
Adoption is the biggest risk and we treat it like one. We monitor Weekly Active Contributors per department from day 1 and intervene with you if it drops below 30% over 2 weeks. If we can't fix it together, we'll refund pro-rata. Honest numbers in your contract.
Do you compete with Glean, Microsoft Copilot, or Vanta?
No. Glean and Copilot are retrieval-first — they help find what's already written. Atlas is outbound-first — it asks questions to capture what's only in people's heads. Vanta does compliance evidence collection; Atlas does operational discovery. Different jobs, different buyers.
Are FDE engagements priced separately?
Yes — each FDE engagement is fixed-fee, agreed upfront based on the pre-drafted SOW. Bonus pricing tied to outcomes is available. Twistag handles delivery; clients can also take the SOW to their own engineering team.
What about white-label for consulting firms?
Not in our current go-to-market. We sell direct to operators, portcos, PE firms, and tech companies. Atlas is the IP, not a channel product. If you're a consulting firm with a specific need, get in touch — we may explore co-engagement on a case-by-case basis.
What does the data residency look like in Europe?
European customers are hosted in Frankfurt by default. We're GDPR-compliant by design, with works council notices ready for Germany and France. DPAs are standard; we don't make you ask twice.
